# YieldMax Product Documentation

## Overview

YieldMax is an autonomous high-yield portfolio management platform designed to eliminate "Value Leakage" in credit card usage through intelligent monitoring and proactive recommendations.

## The Problem

Credit card holders leave significant value on the table due to:
- Suboptimal card selection for spending categories
- Unused credits and benefits that expire
- Inability to track actual ROI on card fees

## The Solution

### Core Components

#### Orchestrator
The central nervous system that monitors real-time spend and lifestyle shifts, coordinating specialized agents.

#### The Scout
- **Purpose**: Maximize reward capture
- **Function**: Monitors spending categories in real-time
- **Action**: Recommends the next best card based on category matching
- **Goal**: Ensure every dollar is optimized for rewards

#### The Enforcer
- **Purpose**: Prevent benefit leakage
- **Function**: Tracks itemized credits and expiration dates
- **Action**: Sends proactive nudges as benefits approach expiration
- **Goal**: Ensure 100% benefit utilization

#### The Auditor
- **Purpose**: Optimize card portfolio
- **Function**: Quantifies realized ROI against annual fees
- **Action**: Delivers "Keep or Cancel" verdicts based on actual performance
- **Goal**: Remove underperforming cards from the portfolio

## Key Metrics

- **Realized Value**: Total rewards and benefits earned
- **Total Costs**: Annual fees and interest charges
- **Net ROI**: Realized value minus total costs
- **Category Optimization Score**: Percentage of spending on optimal cards
- **Benefit Utilization Rate**: Percentage of available benefits used
